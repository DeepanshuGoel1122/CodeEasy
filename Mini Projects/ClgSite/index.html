About IAMR

IAMR Group of Institutions

It gives me immense pleasure to introduce Institute of Technology & Management, Meerut to Engineering as well as Management aspirants. Institute of Technology & Management was established in the year 2008 under the guidelines of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow formerly Uttar Pradesh Technical University, AICTE & UGC under section (2f) with a view of to be a pioneer imparting quality education with student centered approach. The Institute has made great progress in a very short period on time and it has been only possible due to visionary outlook of the management and the core faculty members, who have made strategic efforts to provide quality education to the aspirants of ITM. The ITM equipped with latest technology for improving quality education to bring efficient human resource to the nation. We strictly follow the norms of AICTE. We at ITM offers B-Tech (CSE, ITE, ECE, ME ,CE) / MBA of the highest academic Standard. Our programmes are designed to provide education to students who come from a wide range of education backgrounds. Students from ITM Meerut are adaptable to any working condition making them employable. Here the students receive education relevant to the needs of the Local & Global Industries. It is our endeavor to provide high quality Technical & Management Education which will help our students to excel any field they opt for growing as true professionals. Through B.Tech from ITM you enter the professional world having worked on intensive research projects, studying from experts across the globe and almost a year's industry experience. So, at the end of 4 years you graduate with technical attribute with more than just a degree. Innovation is the base of ITM B-Tech programme as well. The course compromises of lateral integration of a wide variety of knowledge disciplines, while allowing its students to modify a sizeable portion of the course as well. To be in line with demands of the industry, eminent faculty with ample industry experience, regular industry visits and annual internships help students to be better equipped for their careers.
